I'm Wil, and for as long as I can remember I have loved videogames. I started doing games with pen and paper, now I'm starting From Hell Studio to make PC and mobile games with my friends.

Our first game will be a sci-fi point-and-click adventure where you must lead the crew of the spaceship Hope in a crucial intergalactic mission.

Subscribe to our mailing list if you want to know more about Hope and our future games.
